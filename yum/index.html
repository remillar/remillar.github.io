Cheese Ravioli with Lemon Basil Butter Sauce

Serves 4-5

Ingredients

  1. 22 ounces frozen cheese ravioli
  2. 2 half sticks (1/2 cup) butter with olive oil and sea salt
  3. 1 tablespoon minced shallot
  4. 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  5. 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  6. 2 tablespoons chopped basil
  7. Salt and black pepper, to taste
  8. 1/3 cup shredded parmesan cheese, for garnish
  9. Extra basil for garnish, optional
