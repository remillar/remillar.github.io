LASS Season 2013/14

LASS is the Snowsport Scotland initiative established to encourage girls and women to become actively involved in the Scottish snowsports scene. The aim is to get as many girls out there as possible during their weekends at the Cairngorm mountain in the Scottish Highlands. In 2014, LASS emerged from its pilot year hoping to become bigger and better.
