NOTORIOUS

THE BRIEF: Become as famous as possible in 30 hours.

Freddy Taylor of KesselsKramer London returned to Edinburgh College of Art graphic design department to challenge the 2nd and 3rd year students.

In our team of 7 we decided that the easiest way to attract attention would be to create an illegal event but design the advertising campaign in a way that would be believable. We wanted to emphasise the fact that many stories online are believed but are often untrue. We chose to create a fake animal fighting event called Exotic Animal Showdown.

We produced posters, pickets and flyers printed onto bright stock and began distributing them in teams with two cameras following. Crudely made t-shirts with our hashtag also featured. We became innundated with emails, tweets and Facebook messages from the public questioning the show.

At the end of the 30 hours we had reached over 1500 people. After a few days we decided to use the page for good - so we explained who we were and apologised if we offended anyone on our online accounts. We raised Â£100 on a just giving page to help prevent animal cruelty around the world.
