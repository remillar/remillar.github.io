KesselsKramer London

I spent two weeks of my summer living in London and interning at KesselsKramer London. During my time there I worked on several projects with the team and fellow intern James Findlay.

KK Outlet Shop Product

We were set the task of producing something that the KK Outlet shop could sell. My solution was to create an adult ‘Lucky Dip’ based on the ‘goodie bags’ we all recieved at parties when we were children. The dips come in three colours and all the ‘goodies’ correspond to the colour on the outside of the box.

Thanks to KK London for having me.
